The Next Evolution of Labor.
Vanar Generation 1.

Vanar Generation 1 is a general-purpose humanoid robot designed to operate in human environments. It uses human-like movement to perform tasks in the same spaces people work. Vanar is built for work that is repetitive, physically demanding, or unsustainable for humans over long periods. It enables organizations to maintain continuity while people focus on oversight, judgment, and decision-making. Vanar extends human capability by taking on work that should not rely on constant human effort.
